§3.6 · FAQ — CSS-only details/summary, answers ≤300 chars
Does a water softener add value to a home?

Indirectly. It protects the water heater and fixtures — appraisers don't line-item it, but inspectors flag scale damage. Expect protection, not resale premium.

Can I install a softener myself?

If a loop exists, yes — it's two connections and a drain line. No loop means cutting into the main, which most homeowners should price out professionally.
