Frequently asked

Is [Brand] equipment bad?

No — the hardware is generally solid ion-exchange equipment. This page critiques pricing opacity, not the product. You’re paying a sales channel, not a better resin.

Can I buy [Brand] without the in-home visit?

Usually not at list terms — the dealer channel is the product. Some models appear used or via independent installers; warranties often don’t transfer.

Will [Brand] match a competitor’s quote?

Reps commonly have discount authority of 20–40% off the opening number. A written competing quote is the fastest way to find the floor.

What do [Brand] owners say about service?

Service reviews skew positive; price-related complaints dominate the negatives. That pattern — good product, opaque price — repeats across the dealer brands we track.
