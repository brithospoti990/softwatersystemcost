Terms of use

Using this site means accepting these terms. They are written in plain English because terms nobody can read protect nobody.

What this site is

SoftWaterSystemCost is an independent publication about water-treatment costs. It is not a plumbing contractor, a water-treatment dealer, an engineering service, or a laboratory. Nothing here is professional plumbing, legal, financial, or health advice. Our numbers are researched, sourced, and dated — and they are still estimates and published ranges, not quotes. Your project’s real price comes from a written quote by a licensed local professional; your water’s real chemistry comes from your utility’s report or a lab test; your local plumbing code outranks anything written here.

Calculators are estimators

Our interactive tools run arithmetic on sourced figures and clearly labelled assumptions. They exist to help you understand and compare quotes — not to replace them. A calculator’s output is not a promise of price, savings, suitability, or performance, and several of our tools will decline to answer at all where an input (a lab test, a vendor’s price) is missing — that refusal is a feature, and it is also your model for how to treat any single number on the internet, including ours.

Accuracy, corrections, and no warranty

We work hard on accuracy — the build pipeline machine-checks links, tests calculator branches, and dates figures — and prices still float, sources still move, and errors still happen. Content is provided “as is,” without warranty of any kind. Found something wrong? Corrections outrank everything.
